About Me & My Story

I'm a B.Tech and M.Tech student in Mathematics & Computing at IIT Delhi, where I've consistently maintained a CGPA of 9.12 (Department Rank 3). My journey began with a passion for problem-solving that led me to excel in competitive mathematics and physics olympiads, eventually securing an All India Rank of 115 in JEE Advanced.

At IIT Delhi, I've immersed myself in the fascinating intersection of mathematics, machine learning, and quantitative finance. My research interests span across graph neural networks, model checking of concurrent systems, and algorithmic trading strategies. I've worked on cutting-edge projects involving friend recommendation systems using multimodal GNNs, bipartite graph attention networks for personality prediction, and influence maximization in social networks.

Beyond academics, I'm deeply passionate about quantitative finance and algorithmic trading. I've won the QRT Quant Quest Data Challenge, achieving an impressive 3.9 Sharpe ratio, and qualified for nationals in the WorldQuant International Quant Championship. My work on Leverage Rotation Strategies in global markets has shown how mathematical rigor can generate superior risk-adjusted returns.

I'm also an active competitive programmer with an Expert rating on Codeforces (1660) and 1900+ on CodeChef, constantly sharpening my algorithmic thinking. Whether it's optimizing NP-hard problems, designing novel neural architectures, or developing quantitative trading strategies, I thrive on challenges that require deep mathematical insight and innovative solutions.
